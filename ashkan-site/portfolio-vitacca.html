About the Project

Vitacca Ballet is a prestigious dance company known for their innovative performances and dedication to artistic excellence. We were commissioned to create promotional imagery for their upcoming season.

The shoot took place in their rehearsal studio, capturing the dancers in motion and at rest. The images highlight the grace, strength, and emotion that define Vitacca's work, and have been used in their season brochures, website, and advertising campaigns.
